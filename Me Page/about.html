    • A Day In the Life

    A typical weekday for me looks like getting up around 4 AM to workout. Right now I like to do weights and then walk for about 30 minutes. I then head to the kitchen to take my vitamins and supplements. After that I get dressed for I.C.Stars. I've really been enjoying exploring fashion and developing my personal style, so that makes getting dressed everyday more fun for me. Once dressed I make sure that I have everything packed like my supplies and lunch before I make the 30- 45 min commute to the office. I like to give myself a lot of time in the morning because I like to take my time. During the workday I am always learning something new, and that's what I like most about the program. The staff keeps us very busy which is great! Our day is over by 7:30 PM and if I haven't driven myself, my fiance comes to pick me up. I like when we commute together because we're always busy doing our own things, so it's nice that we have a little time together in the morning and afternoon. By the time I get home I am exhaused. Sometimes I do a little studying but most of the time I go straight to bed.
